Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our scholarship program

Can I apply if I'm already receiving other scholarships?

Yes, our scholarships can complement other financial aid. We encourage students to pursue multiple funding sources to fully cover their educational expenses.

Do I need to maintain a certain GPA to keep my scholarship?

Yes, scholarship recipients must maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA and make satisfactory academic progress to continue receiving support.

What expenses does the scholarship cover?

Our scholarships cover tuition, books, uniforms, essential supplies, and for boarding students, living allowances. We aim to remove all financial barriers to education.

Can international students apply?

Currently, our program focuses on local students, but we plan to expand internationally as our foundation grows. Please check back for updates.
