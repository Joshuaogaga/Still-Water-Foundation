Getting Started

Simple requirements to join our volunteer community

Basic Requirements

  • 18 years or older (16+ with guardian permission)
  • Passion for education and helping others
  • Reliable internet connection for virtual activities
  • Commitment to our mission and values
  • Willingness to undergo basic orientation

What We Provide

  • Comprehensive orientation and training
  • Ongoing support from our team
  • Flexible scheduling around your availability
  • Recognition and certificates for your service
  • Opportunity to grow and take on new roles
