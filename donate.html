Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about donating

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Still Water Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records.

How secure is online giving?

Very secure. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with trusted payment processors. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

Can I donate by check?

Yes! Make checks payable to "Still Water Foundation" and mail to: 123 Education Lane, Hope District, City, State 12345

Can I cancel or modify my monthly donation?

Absolutely. You can change or cancel your recurring donation at any time by contacting us at donate@stillwater.org or calling +1 (234) 567-8900.

Will I receive updates on how my donation is used?

Yes! All donors receive our quarterly impact reports via email, showing exactly how contributions are making a difference.
