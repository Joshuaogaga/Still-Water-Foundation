About Still Water Foundation

Like still water that runs deep, our commitment to education transforms lives

Our Story

The Still Water Foundation was born from a simple observation: talent is universal, but opportunity is not. Like the calm surface of still water that reflects infinite possibilities, we believe every child carries immense potential waiting to be discovered.

Founded in 2022, we started with a vision to break down the barriers that prevent underprivileged youth from accessing quality education in Nigeria. What began as a small initiative to help a few students in our local community is growing into something much larger, with plans to expand across Africa.

Our name, "Still Water," symbolizes the quiet strength, depth, and clarity we bring to our mission. Just as still water nurtures life and reflects truth, we provide steady, unwavering support that allows young minds to flourish.

We're just getting started, and every student we reach brings us closer to our bigger vision.
